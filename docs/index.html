Features

Built for your
music video collection.

Every feature is designed around one goal — making your videos look and sound as good as possible with zero friction.

📺

Continuous Playback

Shuffles your library and plays non-stop, like a real TV channel. Set it up once and forget about it.

📡

Local Network Streaming

Share your Mac library over Wi-Fi. Apple TV connects instantly via Bonjour — no setup, no accounts.

🎙️

Voice Search

On Apple TV, press the mic on your Siri Remote and speak a song or artist name. Results appear instantly.

⭐

Favorites & Genres

Star videos to save them. Organize by genre using your folder structure. Favorites sync across all devices.

🎬

Bumper Videos

Insert short bumper clips between videos to give your channel a broadcast TV feel. Shuffle them too.

🎨

Real-time Video Filters

Apply GPU-accelerated CoreImage effects — from subtle color grades to retro CRT and film looks.

🔊

Audio Normalization

Consistent loudness across your entire library. No more reaching for the remote every other video.

📋

Smart Title Parsing

Automatically cleans up filenames — strips "(Official Video)", "(HD)", and other tags for clean display everywhere.

🔒

Completely Private

No accounts, no analytics, no cloud. Your videos never leave your network. Zero data collected. Ever.
