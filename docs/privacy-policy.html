Privacy Policy

MusicTV is built on a simple principle: your data is yours and stays on your devices.

No data collected
The short version

MusicTV does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. Everything stays on your devices and your local network. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

What MusicTV does

MusicTV is a local music video player. It reads video files from a folder you choose, builds a playback queue, and plays them back on your Apple TV or Mac. It can also discover other MusicTV instances on your local Wi-Fi network so you can stream your library to other devices in your home.

Data we collect

None. MusicTV collects no personal information of any kind. There are no user accounts, no sign-ins, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking libraries included in the app.

Local network access

MusicTV uses your local Wi-Fi network to discover and connect to other MusicTV instances running on the same network — for example, a Mac sharing its library to an Apple TV. This communication happens entirely within your home network using Bonjour, a standard Apple technology for local device discovery. No data leaves your network.

Microphone access (Apple TV only)

On Apple TV, MusicTV asks for microphone access to enable voice search. When you press the microphone button on the Siri Remote, your speech is processed locally by the tvOS operating system and converted to text inside the search field. MusicTV does not record, store, or transmit your voice or any audio data.

Your video library

MusicTV reads video files only from the folder you explicitly select. It does not scan your device for other files, access your Photos library, camera, or any other location. On macOS, file access is managed through Apple's security-scoped bookmarks — MusicTV can only read the folder you grant it access to.

Favorites and settings

Favorites you mark and app settings (shuffle, repeat, bumper interval, etc.) are stored locally on your device using standard Apple system storage. This data never leaves your device except when syncing favorites to another MusicTV instance on your local network — and even then, it travels only over your private local network, not the internet.

Third-party services

MusicTV uses no third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash-reporting services. The only frameworks used are Apple's own system frameworks (AVFoundation, SwiftUI, Network, etc.).

Children's privacy

Because MusicTV collects no personal data from anyone, it is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children or adults.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how MusicTV handles data, we will update this page and revise the date below. Given that the app collects no data, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.
