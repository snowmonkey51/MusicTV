How It Works

Quick Start

Up and running in minutes.

MusicTV is designed to stay out of the way. Point it at your videos and it does the rest.

1

Choose your folder

Open MusicTV on your Mac and select the folder where your music videos live. That's all the setup required.

2

Share your library

Flip on "Share Library" and your Mac becomes a local streaming server. Discoverable instantly on your network.

3

Connect on Apple TV

Open MusicTV on your Apple TV. Your Mac appears in the list. Tap to connect and your library loads in seconds.

4

Sit back and watch

MusicTV shuffles and plays your collection in a continuous loop. Your own music video channel, on your TV.

Organizing Your Library

Folders are your genres

MusicTV reads your folder structure and uses it as your genre system. Every folder you create inside your library becomes a genre you can browse and filter by — no tagging or metadata required.

Nesting works too. A subfolder inside a genre folder creates a sub-genre, and MusicTV tracks both levels so you can filter broadly or get specific.

📁 Music Videos/
  📁 Rock/
    📁 Classic Rock/
      🎬 Led Zeppelin - Kashmir.mp4
    📁 Alternative/
      🎬 Radiohead - Karma Police.mp4
  📁 Hip-Hop/
    🎬 Kendrick Lamar - HUMBLE.mp4
  📁 Pop/
    🎬 Dua Lipa - Levitating.mp4

In the sidebar on your Mac, each top-level folder appears as a genre. Tap a genre on Apple TV to filter playback to just those videos. Sub-genre folders narrow it down further.

Files sitting directly in the root of your library — not inside any folder — are treated as uncategorized and always included in the full shuffle.
